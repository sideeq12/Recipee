Classic CheeseCake Recipe

look no further for a creamy and clasic ultra-smooth CheeseCake recipe!. Paired with a buttery graham cracker rust, no one can deny its simple decadence. for the best result, bake in water bath.

INGREDIENT

Graham Cracker crust
  • 1 and 1/2 Cup (150g) gram Cracker crumb(about 10 full sheet garham Cracker)
  • 5 Tablespoons (70g) unsalted butter melted.
  • 1/4 Cup(50g) unsalted butter granulated sugar.
CheeseCake
  • foour 8-ounce blocks (904g) full-fat cream cheese softened to room temperature
  • 1 Cup (200g) Granulated sugar
  • 1 Cup (240g) full-fat sour cream, at room team temperature
  • 1 Tablespoon pure vanilla extract
  • 2 Teaspoons fresh lemon juice (Optional but recommended).
  • 3 large eggs, at room temperature.
  • Topping suggestions : salted caramel, lemon curd, strawberry, topping, chocolate gonache, red wine chocolate ganache, fresh fruit, whipped cream or raspberry sauce (recipe in notes).
YIELDS 12 serving
PREP TIME 45 minutes
YIELDS 12 serving

Instroduction

  • Adjust the track to the lower middle position and preheat over to 350^F(177^C)
  • Using a food processor, pulse the programme crackers in to crumbs. Pour in to medium bowl and stir in sugar and melted butter until combined.(You can also pulse it all together in the food processor.) Mixture will be sandy, press firmly in to the buttom and slightly up the sides of a 9-inch or 10-ince springform pan.No need to greese the pan.
